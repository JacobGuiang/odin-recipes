Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is a pasta consisting of chicken and alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • kosher salt
  • ground pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cup low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlics, minced
  • 8 ounces fetucinni
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan
  • chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice.
  3. Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente.
  4. Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley.
